# HexaCV - SEO Strategy (Research-Backed)

## Pain Points (From Reddit/Quora/Industry Research)

### ATS Anxiety Crisis
- **77% of job seekers** express concern about ATS rejection (Monster 2024)
- **75% of resumes** get rejected by ATS before human review
- **57% quit applications** due to job search frustration (LiveCareer 2025)
- "ATS anxiety is widespread" - resumes getting longer but not clearer

### User Frustrations
1. Don't know what keywords ATS looks for
2. Complex resume builders require signup/payment
3. Freshers struggle with ATS formatting
4. Can't extract keywords from job descriptions easily
5. No feedback on why resume was rejected
6. Too many steps to download resume
